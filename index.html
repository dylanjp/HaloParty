// REQ-01

Halo: MCC

You need Halo: The Master Chief Collection on Xbox or Steam (PC).

CRITICAL: MCC is a launcher. Halo 1, 2, and 3 each install separately from inside it. Open MCC ahead of time and confirm each game is downloaded — don't assume "I have MCC" means you're ready to play Halo 2.

Steam price is $39.99 but it hits 75% off (~$10) regularly. On Xbox it's included with Game Pass Essential or higher, or buy it outright.

// REQ-02

Xbox Account

You need a free Xbox / Microsoft account — yes, even on Steam.

Cross-play invites route through Xbox Live. Steam friends won't get you into the lobby. Xbox friends will.

Make one at xbox.com if you don't have one yet.

// REQ-03

Multiplayer Access

On Xbox console: you need Game Pass Essential to play online. Note: this used to be called "Game Pass Core" — Microsoft renamed it in 2026. Same thing.

On PC (Steam): nothing extra. Just MCC and the Microsoft account.

Cost tip: if you don't already have Game Pass, a $10 sale-priced Steam copy is cheaper than one month of the subscription.

// REQ-04

Cross-Network Play

Turn Cross-Network Play ON inside MCC. Required for our mixed Xbox + PC lobby.

Path: MCC → Settings → Game Options → Cross-Network Play → ON.

Do this once before the party. It's a one-and-done.

// SETUP-PC

Windows / Mac

1. Make a free Discord account at discord.com.

2. Click the glowing button above to join the server.

3. Download the desktop app — better audio than browser.

4. Settings → Voice & Video → pick your mic and click "Let's Check" to test. Pick Push-to-Talk or Voice Activity (your call).

// SETUP-XBOX

Xbox Console

Xbox has native Discord voice on Series X|S and Xbox One:

1. Install Discord on your phone if you haven't.

2. Link Discord to your Microsoft account: Discord Settings → Connections → Xbox.

3. Join the voice channel in Discord mobile, then tap Transfer Voice to Xbox.

Fallback: just use Discord on your phone or PC for voice while playing on Xbox. Works fine. Use headphones.

// SETUP-MOBILE

Mobile Backup

Install Discord from the App Store or Google Play.

Useful as a backup if your PC mic dies, or to keep chatting if you step away from the screen.

Headphones strongly recommended — laptop or phone speakers cause feedback that ruins it for everyone else in the channel.
